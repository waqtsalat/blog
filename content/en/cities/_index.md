## How We Calculate Prayer Times

All city pages use the official **Habous Ministry method** with:
- Fajr angle: 19°
- Isha angle: 17°

This matches the times announced at local mosques and broadcast by Moroccan media.

## Why City-Specific Times Matter

Prayer times vary slightly between cities due to geographic location. The sun's position relative to each city affects when each prayer begins. Our calculations account for:

- **Latitude and longitude** of each city
- **Elevation** adjustments
- **Timezone** settings (GMT+0 or GMT+1 during daylight saving)
- **Local conventions** for Adhan and Iqama times
