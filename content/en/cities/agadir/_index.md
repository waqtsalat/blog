# Prayer Times in Agadir

Agadir, Morocco's premier beach destination and capital of the Souss-Massa region, offers a unique setting for daily prayers. Rebuilt after the devastating 1960 earthquake, modern Agadir combines Berber (Amazigh) traditions with contemporary Moroccan life. The city's southern coastal location provides stable weather conditions ideal for accurate prayer time observations.

## Daily Prayer Schedule

Agadir prayer times follow Morocco's official Habous Ministry method:

| Prayer | Calculation | Time Range |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Fajr** | 19° | 4:10 AM - 6:35 AM |
| **Chourouk** | Sunrise | 5:40 AM - 8:00 AM |
| **Dhuhr** | Solar noon | 12:25 PM - 12:55 PM |
| **Asr** | Shafi'i method | 3:45 PM - 5:15 PM |
| **Maghrib** | Sunset | 6:10 PM - 8:20 PM |
