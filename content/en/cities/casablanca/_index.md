# Prayer Times in Casablanca

Casablanca, known locally as Dar el-Beida, is Morocco's largest city and economic powerhouse. Home to the magnificent Hassan II Mosque, the city represents the modern face of Morocco while maintaining strong Islamic traditions. With over 3 million residents, accurate prayer times are essential for daily life.

## Daily Prayer Schedule

Prayer times in Casablanca follow the official Habous method established by Morocco's Ministry of Endowments and Islamic Affairs:

| Prayer | Calculation | Typical Time Range |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Fajr** | 19° below horizon | 4:00 AM - 6:30 AM |
| **Chourouk** | Sunrise | 5:30 AM - 7:30 AM |
| **Dhuhr** | Solar noon | 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM |
| **Asr** | Shafi'i method | 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM |
| **Maghrib** | Sunset | 5:30 PM - 8:00 PM |
| **Isha** | 17° below horizon | 7:00 PM - 9:30 PM |

Exact times shift daily based on the sun's position and vary by season.

## The Hassan II Mosque

Casablanca's iconic Hassan II Mosque is one of the largest mosques in the world and Morocco's most recognized religious landmark:

- **Capacity:** 105,000 worshippers (25,000 inside, 80,000 on the esplanade)
- **Minaret Height:** 210 meters (tallest in Africa)
- **Features:** Retractable roof, laser beam pointing toward Mecca
- **Location:** On the Atlantic coast, offering stunning ocean views

The mosque's prayer times serve as a reference point for the entire city. Many residents tune in to its Adhan broadcast.

## Major Mosques in Casablanca

Beyond the Hassan II Mosque, Casablanca has numerous significant mosques:

### Mosquée Mohammed V
Located in the city center, this historic mosque serves the commercial district and hosts large Friday congregations.

### Mosquée Lusitania
A prominent mosque in the Maarif district, serving one of Casablanca's busiest neighborhoods.

### Mosquée Ouled Ziane
One of the oldest mosques in Casablanca, located in the traditional medina area.

### Mosquée Salama
Serving the residential districts of Racine and Gironde.

## Casablanca's Unique Characteristics

**Business Hub:** As Morocco's economic center, Casablanca has a large working population that needs flexible prayer time notifications. Many businesses adjust schedules around prayer times.

**Coastal Weather:** The Atlantic influence creates moderate temperatures but can affect visibility for astronomical observations.

**Diverse Population:** Casablanca's cosmopolitan nature includes Muslims from various traditions, making the standardized Habous method essential for consistency.
