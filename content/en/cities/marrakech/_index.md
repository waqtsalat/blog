# Prayer Times in Marrakech

Marrakech, known as the Red City for its distinctive terracotta walls, is one of Morocco's four imperial cities and a center of Islamic learning and culture. The city's iconic Koutoubia Mosque has served as a model for mosque architecture across the Islamic world, including the Giralda in Seville and the Hassan Tower in Rabat.

## Daily Prayer Schedule

Marrakech prayer times follow Morocco's official Habous method:

| Prayer | Calculation Method | Seasonal Variation |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Fajr** | 19° sun angle | 3:45 AM - 5:45 AM |
| **Chourouk** | Sunrise (0°) | 5:15 AM - 7:30 AM |
| **Dhuhr** | Solar transit | 12:15 PM - 12:45 PM |
| **Asr** | Shafi'i shadow method | 3:30 PM - 4:45 PM |
| **Maghrib** | Sunset (0°) | 5:45 PM - 7:45 PM |
| **Isha** | 17° sun angle | 7:15 PM - 9:15 PM |

Marrakech's inland location and proximity to the Atlas Mountains creates slightly different timing patterns compared to coastal cities.

## The Koutoubia Mosque

The Koutoubia Mosque is Marrakech's most famous landmark and spiritual center:

- **Height:** 77 meters minaret
- **Built:** 12th century (Almohad dynasty)
- **Architecture:** Classic Moorish design with intricate geometric patterns
- **Function:** Active mosque serving thousands of worshippers daily

The mosque's Adhan has been broadcast for centuries, and its prayer times serve as the official reference for the entire Marrakech region. The call to prayer from the Koutoubia's minaret remains one of the most moving experiences for visitors and residents alike.

## Notable Mosques in Marrakech

### Ben Youssef Mosque
Historically one of the most important mosques in North Africa, adjacent to the famous Ben Youssef Madrasa. The mosque and its surrounding quarter represent centuries of Islamic scholarship.

### Moulay El Yazid Mosque
A major congregational mosque near the Kasbah, serving the historic royal quarter.

### Sidi Bel Abbas Mosque
Dedicated to the patron saint of Marrakech, this mosque is an important spiritual center for Sufi traditions.

### Bab Doukkala Mosque
Serving one of the main gates of the old city, this mosque serves residents and visitors to the medina.

## Marrakech Prayer Considerations

**Inland Climate:** Marrakech experiences more extreme temperatures than coastal cities. In summer, Dhuhr and Asr prayers occur during the hottest hours, and many residents prefer praying in air-conditioned mosques or homes.

**Tourism Impact:** As Morocco's top tourist destination, Marrakech sees many visitors seeking to understand local prayer practices. Mosques in tourist areas often provide information in multiple languages.

**Atlas Mountains:** The nearby mountains can affect sunrise/sunset visibility at certain times of year, though prayer time calculations account for this.
