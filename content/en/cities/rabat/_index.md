# Prayer Times in Rabat

Rabat, the capital of Morocco, holds special significance for prayer time calculations in the country. As the seat of the Habous Ministry, the official prayer time methods are established here. The city's coastal location on the Atlantic Ocean and the Bou Regreg river creates a unique environment for observing daily prayers.

## Daily Prayer Schedule

Prayer times in Rabat follow the official Habous method with specific sun angles:

| Prayer | Angle/Method | Description |
|--------|--------------|-------------|
| **Fajr** | 19° | Dawn prayer, begins when sun is 19° below horizon |
| **Chourouk** | 0° | Sunrise, end time for Fajr |
| **Dhuhr** | Solar noon | Midday prayer |
| **Asr** | Shafi'i method | Afternoon prayer |
| **Maghrib** | 0° | Sunset prayer |
| **Isha** | 17° | Night prayer |

Times adjust throughout the year based on the sun's position. During summer, Fajr occurs earlier and Isha later, while winter brings later Fajr and earlier Isha.

## Notable Mosques in Rabat

Rabat is home to several historic and architecturally significant mosques:

### Hassan Tower Mosque
The incomplete mosque begun in the 12th century, with its iconic minaret, stands as a monument to Almohad architecture. The adjacent Hassan II Mausoleum contains the tombs of Moroccan kings.

### Kasbah of the Udayas Mosque
Located within the ancient kasbah overlooking the Atlantic, this mosque serves the historic quarter and features traditional Moroccan architecture.

### Sunna Mosque
One of the oldest mosques in Rabat, located in the medina near the central market, serving the local community for daily prayers.

### As-Sounna Mosque
A major congregational mosque in the city center that hosts large Friday gatherings and Eid prayers.

**Administrative Center:** As the capital, Rabat often announces official prayer times that other regions follow.

**University Community:** With Mohammed V University and numerous educational institutions, Rabat has a large student population that relies on accurate prayer schedules.
