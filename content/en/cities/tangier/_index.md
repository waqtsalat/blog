# Prayer Times in Tangier

Tangier sits at the northernmost tip of Africa, where the Mediterranean Sea meets the Atlantic Ocean at the Strait of Gibraltar. As Morocco's primary port city and a historic international zone, Tangier has a unique cultural character. The city's position at the crossroads of continents makes its prayer times among the earliest in Morocco due to its northern latitude.

## Daily Prayer Schedule

Tangier prayer times follow the official Habous method:

| Prayer | Method | Approximate Time |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| **Fajr** | 19° | 3:40 AM - 6:10 AM |
| **Chourouk** | Sunrise | 5:10 AM - 7:35 AM |
| **Dhuhr** | Solar noon | 12:20 PM - 12:50 PM |
| **Asr** | Shafi'i | 3:40 PM - 5:00 PM |
| **Maghrib** | Sunset | 5:50 PM - 8:05 PM |
| **Isha** | 17° | 7:20 PM - 9:35 PM |

Tangier's northern location results in the most extreme seasonal variations in Morocco, with very early Fajr times in summer and very late sunrises in winter.

## Major Mosques in Tangier

The main congregational mosque in the city center, serving thousands of worshippers and acting as the primary reference for prayer times in the region.

### Mosquée Mohammed V
A prominent mosque in the ville nouvelle (new city), serving the modern administrative and business districts.

### Mosquée Al Hoda
Located in the residential areas, this mosque serves the growing suburban communities.

### Kasbah Mosque
Within the historic kasbah overlooking the Strait of Gibraltar, this mosque serves the old medina community.

## Tangier's Unique Position

**Northernmost Major City:** Tangier's latitude of 35.75°N makes it the northernmost major city in Morocco. This results in:
- **Summer:** Very early Fajr (before 4 AM) and very late Isha (after 10 PM)
- **Winter:** Later Fajr (around 6 AM) and earlier Maghrib (around 5:30 PM)

**Coastal Influence:** The meeting of Atlantic and Mediterranean waters creates unique weather patterns that can affect visibility for astronomical observations.

**International Character:** Tangier's history as an international city has created a diverse Muslim community that relies on standardized prayer times for consistency.
