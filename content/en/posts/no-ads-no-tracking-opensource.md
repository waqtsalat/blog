Your prayer app shows an ad. You see a notification asking for data collection. This shouldn't be normal.

## The Problem With Islamic Apps

Many prayer apps make money in ways that harm your worship:

**Ads:**
- Banner ads distracting during prayer
- Pop-ups between prayer times
- Sponsored content mixed with Islamic information
- Making profit from your religious practice

**Tracking:**
- GPS tracking every time you open app
- Device fingerprinting to identify you
- Selling your location data to third parties
- Analytics for advertising targeting

These practices treat your prayer routine as a product to monetize, not a service to support.
