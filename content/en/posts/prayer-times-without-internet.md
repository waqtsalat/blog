Your phone shows no signal. It's time for Maghrib. Do you know when to pray?

## The Connectivity Reality

Morocco has good internet coverage in cities, but connectivity issues happen:

- **Metro and tram**: Dead zones underground
- **Rural areas**: Limited coverage
- **Travel**: Roaming or weak signals
- **Data limits**: Running out of mobile data
- **Network outages**: Temporary service disruptions

When the athan time comes, you shouldn't need to check your internet connection first.

## What Offline-First Means

Offline-first design means the app works without internet by default:

- All prayer calculations happen on your device
- City coordinates are stored locally
- Settings and preferences stay on your phone
- No server calls required for core functionality

WaqtSalat calculates prayer times using mathematical formulas and your location. No internet needed after the initial setup.

## How WaqtSalat Works Offline

**Initial setup:**
1. You select your city (35+ Moroccan cities pre-loaded)
2. The app stores your city's coordinates
3. All calculations use these stored coordinates

**Daily use:**
1. Open the app anytime
2. See today's prayer times instantly
3. No loading, no waiting, no "checking connection"

**Prayer calculations:**
- Uses the Habous method formulas
- Applies Morocco's timezone rules (including DST changes)
- Works for years into the future

## Benefits of Offline Prayer Apps

**Reliability:**
- Works everywhere, every time
- No dependency on external servers
- No downtime during network issues

**Privacy:**
- No data sent to servers
- Your location stays on your device
- No tracking or analytics

**Speed:**
- Instant load times
- No network latency
- Works on older phones

**Battery:**
- No background network calls
- Less battery drain
- More efficient than online apps

## When You Do Need Internet

WaqtSalat requires internet only for:

- Initial download (once)
- App updates (optional)
- That's it

Everything else works offline, forever.

## Progressive Web App

WaqtSalat is a PWA (Progressive Web App):

- Install it like a native app
- Works offline after installation
- Updates automatically when online
- Takes minimal storage space
