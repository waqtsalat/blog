## About This Site

WaqtSalat Blog provides Islamic prayer time information for Morocco, using the official Habous Ministry calculation method.

- **Main App**: https://waqtsalat.github.io/waqtsalat/
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/waqtsalat
- **Language**: Multilingual (English, French, Arabic)
